About Afriski Mountain Ski Festival

This event is an annual
winter tourism festival held at Afriski
Mountain Resort in the Maluti Mountains of Lesotho.

The event celebrates snow sports,
mountain adventure, music, and culture.
Visitors enjoy skiing, snowboarding,
live performances and winter games.

History and Significance

The festival was created to promote
winter tourism in Lesotho and attract
international travellers looking
for unique snow experiences in Africa.

Target Tourists

  • Adventure travellers

  • Ski and snowboard lovers

  • International tourists

  • Young travellers and music fans

  • Local families and students
