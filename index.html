Afriski Mountain Ski Festival

Adventure Tourism Sports.

Event Overview

Afriski Mountain Sports Festival celebrates
outdoor and mountain culture in Southern Africa’s
only true alpine environment. Participants compete
in skiing and snowboarding contests while others
enjoy trail runs and mountain bike races through
high-altitude terrain. The festival often feature
live music, local cuisine, and après-ski gatherings
that combine sport with Lesotho’s unique mountain
hospitality.

Tourists from around the world come
to enjoy skiing, mountain biking,
snow games and Basotho cultural experiences.
