Food and Drinks

Visitors attending the Afriski Snow Event
will enjoy a variety of food and drinks
available throughout the day. All guests who
have purchased a ticket will have access to several food
stalls and winter refreshment stations around the resort.

  1. Hot chocolate and coffee to keep guests warm
  2. Grilled burgers and sandwiches
  3. Traditional Basotho meals
  4. Snacks and desserts
  5. Soft drinks and other refreshing beverages
